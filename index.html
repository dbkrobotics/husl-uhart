Welcome to HUSL Robotics Group @ UHart!

The Hartford Ubiquitous Systems for Lives (HUSL) lab is driven by a mission to save lives and improve human well-being in times of crisis, disaster, and beyond. We explore advanced robotics technology, Artificial Intelligence (AI), and ubiquitous computing to create innovative devices and systems that can assist people when they need it most.

 

By merging cutting-edge research with real-world applications, HUSL strives to build a safer, more resilient future—one where technology seamlessly enhances everyday life and empowers communities worldwide.
